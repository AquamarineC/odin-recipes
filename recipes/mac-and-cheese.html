Mac and Cheese

Description

After many trials and errors, I reached a decent balance of the most simple to make and my daughters are both loving it.

This is my homemade Mac and Cheese. Nothing fancy, but easy to make, easy to measure, and easy to standardize

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni Of course lol
  • Cheese (Yes I tried many kinds, but ended up using Medium Cheddar exclusively.)
  • Homo Milk
  • Salted butter: It adds flavour, but the main purpose is the thicken the milk
  • Sea salt

Steps:

  1. Grate the cheese. I know it is lots of work, but I see it as necessary because we can then melt the cheese properly in the pan
  2. Boil the noodles, drain, and set aside
  3. Using the same empty boiling pan, add milk, dump all the grated cheese inside and the keep the heat low
  4. Now see the cheese melt in the milk. Add butter accordingly to thicken the sause. or add back more milk otherwise. Very resilient to mistakes, so that one can hardly screw up
  5. Add salt to your liking, and dump the macaroni in.
  6. Keep stiring until macaroni is all coated with thick cheese sause
  7. Taste it, because you can still add salt at this stage. Again, hard to screw up

Not the fanciest Mac and Cheese dish, but simple and easy. I use a whole back of a Macaroni and a whole package of cheese, so I also do not have to measure. Easy to stock and inventory. Used the least amount of supply and get it up and running on moments notice
