Carrot Meat Rice

Await picture of my own dish

Description

Again, the idea is the simple the better, the less ingredient the better. This is lunch for kids, but can also stand alone as a formal dinner dish.

Ingredients:

  • Ground meat (We only use ground beef for personal preference.)
  • A bag of carrot(3 lb bag)
  • Sea salt
  • Oil. A recommendation only: DO NOT CONSUME SEED OIL!!!
  • Cooking wine if cannot stand the smell from the ground meat
  • Rice of course. Plain rice

Steps:

  1. Wash, pill, and grate the carrot into small dices. This is really the most hardworking part of the dish, unless you also ground your own meat.
  2. Heat the pan, put in quite a bit of oil
  3. After oil is heated, dump in the ground meat, break it apart and cook it. Add the cooking wine if you need to
  4. Add salt to the meat to your liking, then dump in the carrot.(No need to flip dishes back and forth, in and out. Ready in one go)
  5. Keeps stiring until mixed and carrot coated with oil for about 2 min, then add hot water to cover the evenly distributed dish
  6. Let it cook for some time, because it is ready when the extra water evaporate. By now, everything is fully cooked and carrot is tender and juicy

I like to cook big portion for it makes a wonderful leftover.
